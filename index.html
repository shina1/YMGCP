ABOUT US

We unite the youths through peace advocacy, community and tolerance-enhancing activities.

Young Minds for Good Stewardship, Community and Peace Building (YMGCP) is a Non-Governmental Organization (NGO) with the goal of ensuring a civilized society where the citizens will be well informed about the activities of their government and involved in the decision making process. In summary, the NGO looks forward to a Nigeria and a world of peaceful co-existence with transparent governments that have the will and zeal to serve their people. The idea of this organization was conceived by Abdur- Rahman Omokunmi Olalekan, a legal practitioner and a youth leader who has a vision and mission to rescue the political, social and religious instability currently being experienced in several countries around the globe, Nigeria being a major case study, and to unite the youths through peace advocacy, community and tolerance-enhancing activities. Hence, YMGCP was conceived and birthed in September 2019 to proffer solutions to these problems.
